##### Steps to make Beef noodle curry:

1. Step 2            Add the red curry paste and stir to combine
1. Step 3            Put the creamed coconut in a wok over a medium heat and let it melt into a liquid. Once it has melted increase the temperature slightly and once it starts to bubble add the red curry paste mixture.
1. Step 4            Add the honey, fish sauce and soy sauce and bring the mixture to the boil.
1. Step 5            Add the chicken stock and kaffir lime leaves and simmer for about 15 mins
